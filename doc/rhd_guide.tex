\begin{section}{Gamma-ray binaries}
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Increase refinement by only one level at each restars
\item decrease nremap to 2 if restart crashes
  \item The mask around the pulsar should have enough resolution to
    avoid pressure problems. For v=.99, for lbox=8, lmax=11 is a
    minimum.
\item The first body in the namelist is the ``star'', meaning that the
  passive scalar=1 in its atmosphere.
  \end{itemize}
